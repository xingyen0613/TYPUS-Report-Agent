## SUI Basket Drag Limits mTLP as Fee Discount Compresses Returns

mTLP returned -2.13% on the week. To put that in context: holding the same mix of assets that back mTLP — approximately 36% SUI and 64% USDC — would have returned roughly -1.76% over the same period. mTLP's return came in slightly below that, by about 0.37 percentage points. The three-factor breakdown explains why:

- **Basket effect**: SUI's ~-5% weekly decline, applied to mTLP's 36% SUI basket weight, contributed roughly -1.76% in downward pressure.
- **Fee income**: +0.12% — modest in absolute terms, partly reflecting the compressed fee rates from the ongoing promotion.
- **Counterparty PnL**: +0.25% — traders' net losses this week flowed directly into the pool as gains.

Fee income and counterparty contributions together provided +0.37% of support, but fell just short of fully offsetting the basket headwind. As the fee promotion concludes, the fee contribution is expected to normalize, providing a clearer picture of mTLP's underlying earnings capacity at current volume levels.

iTLP-TYPUS also returned -2.13% this week. As a 100% USDC-denominated product, its return reflects fee income and counterparty dynamics without any basket exposure — the same two tailwinds applied here, partially offset by other protocol-level factors.
